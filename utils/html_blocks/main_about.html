About

From taking apart electronics as a child, building HTML/CSS web pages in elementary school, to inadvertently learning BASIC in 7th grade building games on his TI-83 calculator, Trey has been fascinated with technology from a young age. In high school, Trey studied college level computer science and learned the fundamentals of electronics design.

Trey graduated with a degree in Electrical Engineering, Digital Signal Processing in 2013 and was a top experimental researcher at his university's Wireless Communications Laboratory. While his studies focused largely on math, physics, and theory, Trey honed his practical skills on the side building embedded systems, MCU-based web servers, and learning Python for non-resource constrained jobs to bask in its simplicity.

Trey is a lifelong learner and has worked with a variety of technologies in the enterprise software and semiconductor industries during his professional career. He served as a technical advisor for wireless technologies, edge AI, advanced sensors, and microcontrollers in the semiconductor industry. In the software world, he's helped architect custom search and AI solutions for Fortune 500 companies in various sectors as well as government agencies.

Trey served as Chief Operating Officer for the Mindbreeze Corporation, running their US branch. He is currently an independent consultant and advisor for generative AI solutions.
